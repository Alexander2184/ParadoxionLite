Paradoxion Lite

Rules

Your goal is to clear board from all items. To do so, you can place items in your inventory. Items react when they form combinations, clearing themselves from the board and pushing other items. Items push that way may form other combinations, pushing yet another items and so on.

It's OK to have items in the inventory left when the board is empty, but having empty inventory without clearing the board is a loss.
